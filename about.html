a little bit about us ...

Here at what's for dinner? we understand that at the end of a long day, trying to decide what to have for dinner can feel like a chore. We’ve been there too!

Our modern lives are fast-paced, busy and convenience can be temping. With just a few minutes and a phone, you could order a take-away from your local favourite and your problem would be solved, right? Maybe as a treat now and again but you can’t beat a home cooked meal.

This is why we love fresh cooking:

  • Cheaper
  • Potentially quicker than the delivery person
  • Can bring together friends and family
  • Fills your kitchen with tasty smells
  • Improves cooking skills and confidence
  • Uses up the questionable broccoli at the back of the fridge, reducing food waste

You know you want to have a go. Click the start button and we’ll give you some tasty dinner ideas.
